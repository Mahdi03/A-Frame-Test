Instructions:
  1. On PC, use WASD or Arrow Keys To Move Around.
  2. On PC, use the Mouse to look around.
  3. On Mobile, Just tilt your phone to look around (VR Headsets Help For This)
  4. Just Hit The Ball With your Body To Kick It.
  5. Try To Make It Into The Goal, If You Make It, the score total goes up.
  6. Have Fun!!!
